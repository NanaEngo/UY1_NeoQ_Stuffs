## Research Project Components

1. **Titre** – Clear and impactful
2. **Introduction** – Context and importance
3. **Question de recherche** – Specific scientific question
4. **Problématique** – Why this matters
5. **Objectifs** – General and specific goals
6. **Revue de littérature** – State of the art
7. **Hypothèses** – Testable predictions
