## Internship Structure

### M1 Internship
- **Duration**: 2-3 months
- **Objective**: Apply theoretical knowledge to practical problems
- **Deliverables**: Report, presentation

### M2 Internship
- **Duration**: 4-6 months
- **Objective**: Independent research project
- **Deliverables**: Thesis, defense

---

## Guidelines

1. **Before starting**: Define objectives with supervisor
2. **During**: Weekly progress reports
3. **After**: Submit report and present findings
